3 ways to make a company’s product competitive

According to the business life cycle,a company that has gone through the stage of establishment, growth and maturity ,in the stage of post-maturity, it will face three different situations, renewal, steady state or decline in performance.In order to avoid the serious consequences of the companies’ declining performance, bankruptcy and takeover, the company must take some measures to make its products more competitive.
In the following article, I will mention three ways to make a companies’ products more competitive and why these methods are important.

The first method is to build customer loyalty to the brand ,and
building customer loyalty has many subdivisions.To improve customer loyalty, the company can cultivate customer dependence through more communication with customers and show the customers that the company is considerate of them.At the same time, satisfaction and loyalty are always in direct proportion, so the company should improve the quality of products, to a greater extent to satisfy customers, so as the customers will make the next purchase.
Customers loyalty is necessary because
with a certain amount of customer loyalty, customers will give priority to the company they once bought products from when choosing new products.

The second method is to attract investment partners, or to sell some of the shares.Getting an investment partner and selling a stake means the company has more free money to spend on its operations.
In January 2017, McDonald’s business in the Chinese mainland and Hong Kong was acquired by a new company which is invested by CITIC Group, CITIC Capital and Carlyle Group for 20.8 billion dollar. CITIC Group and CITIC
Capital
together have 51％ of the share in the new company and Carlyle group has 28% of the share in the new company.【paraphrased
from sina financial news report
20170110】In the past three years, McDonald’s has opened more than 1,000 stores in mainland China and Hong Kong after being acquired by a Chinese company,
both revenue and earnings have increased substantially.【paraphrased from the
announcement of CITIC Group
20200303】Finding a partner or saling equity is important because they are the quickest and the most effective way to generate high corporate returns in short period, which works well when companies are desperate for money to compete.

The last method is to make innovations that appeal to consumers.
As mentioned before, McDonald’s
and many western fast food restaurants like McDonald’s, which have produced many new products in China that are not available in other countries
such as
rice porridge, soybean milk, Fried dough sticks and sichuan spicy hamburger.
Meanwhile,
the ingredients used to a large extent also cater more to Chinese people’s
tastes. They often introduce new products and new flavors
to keep consumers fresh as well.

The same went for the famous Apple Inc. In 1996, Apple Inc
faced a series of losses. By 1997, the company had hired jobs back, he had made some innovations, and the
revenue of Apple Inc had increased significantly.（This is from a book I read
about Jobs, but I haven’t found the exact data here, because I haven’t found the
revenue of apple company before 2013, so I first take the parentheses and expand
it over here, and then I’ll make up the data after I find
it.）It’s important to keep innovating, because times are changing, technology is changing and many of the original products are not suitable for modern people, so the products have to keep up with the pace of The times in order to be more competitive.

To sum up, seven ways to make it more competitive. The first is to build customer trust and loyalty. The second is the collaboration and the third is multi-innovation.
Of course, these three methods are not the only ways to keep the compan’s income stable. In today’s pluralistic
and
competitive society, only by constantly taking different measures can the company become more competitive.
