My homework and comments from the teacher

Comments on Judy’s Unit 1, Session 3 Homework

Dr Andrew Hunt June 2020

【requirement of the homework】

Homework

On one side of A4 paper

An electronics firm currently has a production choice between 10 smartphones or 4

televisions.

❖ Calculate and show the production possibility curve.

❖ On 1 single A4 page. Discuss some of the ways that the electronics firm could increase the production possibility for these choices by 50%.

Submit by 30 May 2020

【Homework】

Because there are finite limits to all the resources, the company has to make choice between making 10 smartphones or 4 televisions. However, with scarce resources, satisfying one want often leads to another want being sacrificed, or taken away. Each want an have a cost applied, which is opportunity cost. In this case, if the producer chose to manufacturing 10 smartphones, the profit of producing 4 televisions is the opportunity cost. In order to increase the production possibility for these choice by 50% (15 smartphones or 6 televisions), the production possibility frontier should

expand.

（Graph 1）

There are some ways to expand the ppf:

1.capital investment

2.increasing workforce

3.automation

4.improving workforce skills

（Graph 2）

【Comments】

Content

• The homework considers many of the key elements concerning production possibility and suggestions on how to increase production, with some useful diagrams.

• The homework should have been provided as an essay, to enable the discussion elements to be properly considered.

• Overall the essay would probably be given a Low Pass.

Academic Writing

• The homework was provided as detailed notes. There is no structure to the essay, showing clearly defined introduction, body and conclusions.

• Need to include Your name, Topic discussed, and Submission date on every page. When given a choice of topics, it is always useful to include the relevant

homework title before the start of the essay.

• There were no citations in the homework. All key points, or potentially

controversial statements, theories, statistics, and descriptions should be

supported by references.

More detailed comments have been shown in the essay below [In Blue]

•[A brief introduction providing a summary of the question would have been useful.]

•[The diagram should have a title. It should also be introduced and explained in the essay itself. It would also be useful to explain the PPC and the boundaries it defines.]

•[It would also be useful to include a short discussion explaining that production to the right of the curve is currently impossible. Also that the electronics firm can produce any combination within the curve. But as production moves further to the left of the curve it becomes increasingly inefficient.]

•[It may be good to indicate that capital investment can provide machinery, research,

computers etc. to enable automation which can increase production.]

•[It may also be useful to explain that some of these may be introduced by the company,

the workforce or the government.]

•[Need to explain how each of the factors of production can be achieved and used to improve production.]

•[It would also be useful to include a discussion on economies of scale and how they

can improve production.]

•[No conclusions]

• [No citations or references]
