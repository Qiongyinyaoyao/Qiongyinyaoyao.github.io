one way of measuring business growth (written in 2020.4.11)

Posted on 2020-07-05

Comments on Judy’s Unit 1 Session 1 Homework Dr Andrew Hunt April 2020

Homework

On one page of A4

Select 1 method of measuring businesses and define it.

Discuss how businesses in China could be measured using this method.

Show how this method can be used to measure business growth in China.

Include sources and references.

To be submitted by 11 April 2020

Comments

Content

· Some of the key points including the discussion different types of growth, and how they can be used to measure businesses.

· A few key elements were missing, such defining the measures of growth, and a clear conclusion were barely covered.

· Overall the essay would probably be given a pass.

Academic Writing

· Need to clearly identify all pages of the essay. Each page should include at least your name (or student number), date of submission, Module or department.

· There are few references in this essay. All key points, or potentially controversial statements, theories, statistics, and descriptions should be supported by references. Each reference can be used more than once in the essay to support relevant statements.

· All citations in the essay text should include: Author surname, Year of publication, for example (Cook, 2017) Whereas, references should always take the form of Author surname, Year published, Title, Publisher. You also need to include a little more information in the citation for quotes: Author, Year, Page number.

· In a few cases, the homework included the personal (I, myself etc.). Academic essays are always in the third person.

English Language

· Overall, the written English was acceptable. Although the objectiveness of academic English was sometimes lacking.

· There were a few unclear or poor phrases and incomplete statements, which could cause confusion and lower marking.

Recommendations

· Once the essay is complete, review it and check against the question, to ensure all elements have been clearly answered.

· Ensure all essays include relevant citations (and references) wherever possible.
