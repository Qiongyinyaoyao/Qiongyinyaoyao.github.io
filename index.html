Judy-----How to improve your academic English

Posted on 2020-05-28

Academic style

1)making tentative statements

2)objective

3)use of passive voice

4) nominalizations

5)formality

6)discipline-specific vocabulary

Essay structure and layout

1.Introduction—–introduce the reader to your topic

a)The Hook——to draw the reader into reading the rest of the essay ( eg:
According to XXX )

b)The background information——mention the background

c)The thesis——mention the main idea of the essay and state your position
on the issue

2.Body paragraph (5-8sentences)

a)Topic sentence——introduce the main idea of the paragraph

b)The argument (4-6sentences)——state and explain the argument. The
sentences should be clear and in logical order

c)The conclusion sentence—– to restate and wrap up the paragraph

3.Conclusion

a)The transition sentence——this sentence needs to restate the
issue/topic you are giving about and your opinion

b)Summarize the arguments—–restate your 3 main arguments in 2-3 sentences

c)Restate your thesis—–wrap up your essay with one strong sentence that
is your thesis re-worded

Appropriate use of citations and references

citation—–In the essay, just indicate the surname of the author/journal
(indicate “ unknown “if the author is unknown) and the date of publication of
the book/journal/website

1.do not just use one citation at the end of the paragraph

2.provide citation when you change ideas

3)use citations to support your own ideas

4)take advantage of your own writing to include citations

[Quotation is to quote directly or indirectly what someone else has said.
Citation is to describe what someone else has said in one’s own words.

But both have to be quoted, quotation is to indicate where the sentence is on
that page, and citation is to indicate where it came from.]

reference—– After the essay, more detailed information should be included
(such as :author’s surname, initial years, title of book, edition of number,
where published, publisher…)

1)use a standard format, such as Harvard Style

2)are listed at the end of the essay

3)are sorted by alphabetical order

My_biggest_academic_achievement

Posted on 2020-05-25

My biggest academic achievement.

Since I am still in high school I have not had the opportunity to achieve much academically. If I had to say, my biggest academic achievement so far would be the 45th place in the world in Fermat Contest in the first year of high school.

I did not actually take part in many of these international mathematic competitions in high school. I took part in Fermat Contest and Euclid Contest, but I did not do well in Euclid Contest at that time. I wanted to take the contest again this year, but due to covid-19, all the international mathematic contests cancelled the testing center in our school. So my ranking in the Fermat Contest was probably my biggest academic achievement.

Before the contest, I did not do a lot of preparation, and to be honest, I did not know how to prepare because that was the first time I had taken part in such a contest. I just did several past papers that the teacher gave us as to a practice. Although it is said that the content of Fermat Contest is relatively basic compared to that of Euclid Contest, I still found that I could not answer all the questions correctly and quickly when I was practicing by myself.

In fact, I was quite nervous at the beginning of the real contest which made me feel as if I could not answer many questions (but later I relaxed and worked on them). And I remembered very clearly that the time was very tight and there were some difficult questions that were worth very high marks at the end of the paper, which made me panic. Fortunately, all of the questions were multiple choice questions, and sometimes I was able to compare the choices one by one to the question and try to get the right answer.

There was a long gap between the results and the end of the contest. On the day that the ranking was revealed, one of my friends saw the ranking first She came over and told us that I had got first place in the school. I was really surprised and delighted and I was even happier when I learned my global rankings. Later our school put the grades and rankings of all the students who got good grades onto our school’s Official Account in WeChat.

I think that achievement, may not only help me when I apply to university in the future, it has made me more confident in myself and more interested in mathematics.
