Posted on 2020-03-25

I was cheated by a swindler while shopping online the other day :（

The swindler told me that she could sell me an out-of-print book which was written by one of my favorite authors, and now the author has stopped writing so I can buy the book as soon as possible.

The swindler found me on weibo and asked me if I wanted to buy the book. She claimed that she wanted to sell the book because she didn’t like the author any more and asked me if I would buy the book. After I expressed my intention to buy it, she asked me to add her QQ friends, and in QQ she took pictures of the books to me (later I realized that the pictures of the out-of-print book she sent me were probably stolen from others).

After making me believe that she really had the out-of-print book, she proposed to use alipay to conduct transactions, and told me that alipay is real-name system, if I found that she is a fraud, I can find her through real-name authentication at any time.( I foolishly believed her!!!) Then I couldn’t get in touch with her after I paid. :（

Later, I found other victims of her fraud on weibo and found that she used the same tricks to deceive many people. Moreover, the payment code of alipay she gave me at that time belonged to another victim. According to the victim, the swindler told her: “I will receive a sum of money, but there is something wrong with my alipay. Can you collect the money for me and transfer it to me through WeChat?” (that is, even if I found which person I transferred my money to through alipay, the receiver is not the swindler herself.) So the victim took the money I gave to her and transferred it to the swindler through WeChat.

Later we learned that the fraud had been cheating since 2017 and that more than 90 victims were known to have been duped by the swindler and the swindler got more than 100000 yuan. Moreover the swindler continued to work in the same way (asking victims to transfer money via QQ/WeChat/alipay to sell nonexistent out-of-print books).

Although the total amount of money involved is very high, it is a pity that the amount of fraud in each case does not reach the amount of fraud case. Most importantly, because the scammers have been stealing other people’s identities and the identities of their victims. Among all the victims, no one can know the real name and address of the swindler at present, so even if the case is made, it is difficult to detect. I have to say, the swindler’s anti-detection ability is really high. So some victims even wondered if the scammer were a fraud ring.

After I learned that I had been cheated, I spent two days in a state of anxiety, and did not dare to tell my parents, because I was cheated 300 yuan, although not very much, but not a small amount. At the same time, I also in self-reproach – I usually are very careful and cautious, also do not easily believe strangers, why just this time to see the thing in his heart, inexplicable to become stupid, cheated.

However, as I always kept things in mind, it really affected my mood, and I was very inefficient in class in those days, so I finally told my mother. My mother didn not scold me after she heard what I said. She told mo that although I was cheated this time, but this was the equivalent of spending 300 yuan to purchase a lesson and a life experience – one is, must not believe the strangers on the network easily, another is that transaction must go through a formal platform.

NumPy and Pandas

Posted on 2020-03-21

It’s been a few months since I started learning Python. Well, in addition to learning Python, some time ago I also learned how to use the NumPy and Pandas.
I want to briefly talk about the feeling of learning NumPy and Pandas.

As far as I am concerned, using the two mumerical libraries are not much different from just using Python directly, but with the help of them,processing data is more efficient and covenient, because a lot of the data processing was set up by previous people.

Although both are data-processing packages, there are also differences between NumPy and Pandas .

Personally, I think NumPy focuses more on the numerical calculation and the expansion of array dimensions（especially especially in the case of matrix, this it can be very useful.）. While Pandas is more useful in tabular data structure.

The above should be my most direct feelings towards the two libraries. In fact, a few more specific differences, I may not say up, because now I am not particularly proficient in programming yet. All in all, with the help of these two libraries, writing programs is obviously easier.

Matilda

Posted on 2020-01-22

In the evening, my parenys took me to watch the musical Matilda

This musical was recommended by one of my classmate. She is a loyal fan of musicals and she had told me that this musical had a great staff.

I went to the theatre,read the brochure handed at the door of the theatre and found out that the musical was provided by the Britain’s Royal Shakespeare Company. The choreogtaphers, composers and other staff of the musical also seemed to be very good.

I also learned from the brochure that the novel matilda was written by Roald Dahl, whose novels Charlie and the chocolate factory and Fantastic Mr. Fox I read when I was a kid. These made me look forward to the musical.

In the musical ,my favourite part was Ms Honey,Matilda and some other her classmates in school palying on the swings. The background music was When I grow up which the actors sing while standing or sitting on a swing. The chorus of children’s voices was accompanied by the voice of Ms Honey and the sweet voice of Matilda ,which was very intoxicating,

The acting, singing and dancing of the actos were really perfect! The little actress who played Matilda was very cute , and her acting skill was excellent and sophisticated.

I think it’s the best play I have seen so far.
